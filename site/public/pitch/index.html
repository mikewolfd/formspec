  • WCAG 2.1 AA + Section 508 — accessibility in the behavior layer, not bolted on. Every adapter inherits it.
  • USWDS ships built-in — or plug in any design system through the headless adapter architecture.
  • Submit once, output everywhere — the mapping engine transforms data into every format your systems need.
